BLACKPINK

Bio


Blackpink is a South Korean girl group founded by YG Entertainment in 2016. Their first debut single album Square One was released including the songs "Boombayah" and "Whistle". Their fan page is named "Blink" as a portmanteau of "Black" and "Pink".
In 2018, the song "Ddu-Du Ddu-Du" by Blackpink climbed to the top of the Gaon Digital Chart for three consecutive weeks and was also the most viewed online video on Youtube within 24 hours. Some of the other famous songs by Blackpink are "Kill this love", "How you like that", "Play with fire",...
